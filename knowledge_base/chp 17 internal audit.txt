📚 LECTURE FULL EXPLANATION:
🔹 Internal Auditor vs External Auditor
Internal Auditor: Company ka part, independent nahi, saal bhar company mein hota hai.
Reporting kis ko hoti hai: Management ya Audit Committee.
Multiple areas ka audit karta hai: payroll, sales, processes, etc.
Focus: internal controls design aur compliance check.
External Auditor: Independent, financial statements pe opinion deta hai.
💡 Example: Internal auditor payroll process ko check karta hai ke tax deductions sahi hain ya nahi.
🔹 Internal Audit Process
Planning: Systematic approach – saal ke month-wise plan.
Example: January – payroll audit, February – sales process audit.
Testing Controls:
Check if controls operate as designed.
Focus: risk assessment for audit strategy.
Reporting:
Internal audit report banati hai issues aur management response ke saath.
Report includes: area, control definition, management response, and time to correct.
💡 Example: Payroll audit mein tax deduction wrong → internal audit report, management response aur turnaround time specify.
🔹 Audit Scope & Sampling
Internal audit ka scope poora year cover karta hai, multiple areas.
Sampling use hoti hai, lekin full risk assessment ke basis pe.
Focus: effective internal controls design ensure karna.
💡 Note: Internal audit financial statements pe opinion nahi deta; ye reporting aur control assessment ke liye hota hai.
🔹 Internal Audit vs Operational Compliance Scope
Pehle samjho dono ka scope kya hai: internal audit aur operational compliance.
Internal audit ka focus: financial statements aur internal controls ko review karna, assurance provide karna.
Operational compliance ka focus: operations efficiently chal rahe hain, controls properly operate ho rahe hain.
💡 Example: SCCP ya ECP ki taraf se notices aate hain, inka follow-up ka kaam internal audit department karta hai.
🔹 Internal Audit Department Role
Internal audit department assistant ka kaam manage karta hai, sari notices aur compliance checks handle karta hai.
Internal audit ka objective: financial statement assurance provide karna, operational efficiency ensure karna.
Internal audit control design aur operation check karta hai, par control ko implement karna management ka kaam hai.
💡 Practical Example: Payroll process audit
Internal audit check karta hai ki tax deduction aur employee salary contracts correct hain ya nahi.
Report banate hain aur management ko submit karte hain.
🔹 Checklists & Documentation
Internal audit team checklist banati hai notices aur compliance tasks ke liye.
Sab accounts submit ho jani chahiye before AGM.
Reports prepare ki jaati hain, jismein non-compliance aur corrective actions note kiye jaate hain.
💡 Example: Listed company ke 4 quarterly meetings aur notices ki monitoring.
🔹 Audit Committee Interaction
Audit committee independent board sub-committee hoti hai.
Internal audit reports audit committee ko submit karte hain, aur committee independent chairman ke through review hoti hai.
Internal audit employees company ke internal staff, lekin report independent chairman ke paas jati hai.
💡 Key Point: Independence ensure karna zaruri hai, taake internal audit unbiased action le sake.
🔹 Reporting & Follow-Up
Internal audit report banata hai aur audit committee ke through management tak issues communicate hotay hain.
Non-compliance issue pe management response include hota hai, aur turnaround time specify kiya jata hai.
Internal audit ka scope financial statements pe opinion nahi dena, bas controls aur processes review karna.
💡 Example: Credit limit issues ya payroll tax deduction errors document karna.
🔹 Independence & Appointment
Internal auditor employee hota hai, report independent chairman ko submit karta hai.
Audit committee ka chairman independent hota hai, taake governance aur unbiased reporting maintain ho.
Appointment ka process: Board appoint karta hai, internal audit ki reporting audit committee ke through hoti hai.
💡 Key Difference: Internal audit operational review aur control understanding karta hai, audit committee independent governance aur oversight provide karti hai.
🔹 Systematic Approach & Documentation
Internal audit systematic approach follow karta hai, jaise checklists, agendas, minutes, aur documentation of compliance.
Purpose: report prepare karna, responsibilities highlight karna, evidence collect karna.
Har department apne issues report karta hai aur bonus ya evaluation bhi performance ke hisaab se hoti hai.
1. Independent Chairman ka Role:
Independent chairman poore issues ko dekhta hai aur ok karta hai.
Company me uska koi stage nahi hota aur na hi company me kaam karta hai.
Appointement: Board karta hai.
Independence ensure ki jati hai taki chairman ka company se koi personal connection na ho.
Agar chairman aur CFO dost hain, independence ka issue aa jata hai.
Purpose: Audit committee ka purpose maintain hota hai, governance strong rehti hai.
2. Governance & Control:
Governance ka aim: Controls ko properly design aur operate karwana.
Control evaluation aur examination: Check kiya jata hai ki controls correct purpose ke liye design aur operate ho rahe hain.
Focus: Management ne jo controls lagaye hain, unki accuracy aur appropriateness assess karna.
Independent approver: Example – Credit approval ki responsibility independent person ke paas hoti hai.
3. Internal Audit Function & Resources:
Internal audit function ke liye sufficient resources zaruri hain.
Staff knowledgeable hona chahiye – financial reporting aur audit framework ka proper knowledge.
Staff qualification: Professional degree, audit knowledge hona chahiye, BA pass ya sirf academy qualification enough nahi.
4. Using Internal Audit Work (“Using the Work of Internal Auditors”)
Agar internal audit ne controls test kiye hain, to auditor unka kaam apne working paper me include kar sakta hai.
Purpose: Evidence gather karna aur risk assessment ke liye use karna.
Auditor control testing ka result directly use karta hai, lekin audit opinion dena internal auditors ka kaam nahi.
Condition: Auditor ko ensure karna hoga ki internal audit ka kaam properly documented aur reliable ho.
5. Documentation & Conditions:
Documentation me har discussion aur test record hota hai.
Internal audit ka kaam sirf assist karta hai, auditor ke liye evidence provide karta hai.
Auditor ko assess karna hota hai ki internal audit ka kaam sahi hai aur use karne layak hai.
Limitations: Agar conditions aur events meet nahi hotay, to internal audit work ko use nahi kiya ja sakta.
Topic: Using the Work of Internal Audit (IA) Function
Key Points:
Purpose:
Understand when and how to use IA’s work as part of our audit procedures.
Ensure clarity between using the IA function’s work vs. using their staff as direct assistants.
Options for Use:
Using IA Function Work:
Leverage the IA’s completed work as evidence in our audit.
Does not change ultimate responsibility of the auditor.
Using IA Staff (Direct Assistance):
IA staff can help perform audit procedures under auditor’s supervision.
Auditor remains responsible for review and conclusions.
Conditions/Pre-conditions:
Check if IA’s work is reliable and suitable for use.
Evaluate IA’s competence, objectivity, and scope of work.
Confirm documentation supports IA work being used.
Use only in areas where it is allowed and appropriate.
Decision Process:
Step 1: Decide whether to use IA function work or IA staff.
Step 2: Determine the areas, extent, and procedures where IA work/staff will be used.
Step 3: Review the IA work to ensure it meets audit objectives.
Responsibilities:
Ultimate responsibility remains with the external auditor.
Auditor must review and evaluate the results before relying on IA work.
Documentation:
Record the decision on whether IA work or IA staff is used.
Include rationale, scope, and limitations.
Maintain clear evidence for audit trail.
Assessment/Checklist:
Competence of IA function/staff
Objectivity and independence
Scope and reliability of IA work
Areas where IA work/staff can be used
Documentation and review procedures
Lecture- 2

📚 LECTURE FULL EXPLANATION:
Internal vs External Audit: 🕵️‍♂️
Internal audit appointment: Management ya company decide kar sakti hai. 🏢
External audit: Independent hota hai, internal audit independent nahi. 🔍
Internal audit ka scope: decide hota hai kaunsa area, kab audit karega. Assistant bhi help karta hai. 🤝
Material aur thresholds: Internal audit ke paas thresholds hotay hain, jo evidence report ke liye use hote hain. 📊
Reporting: 📑
Audit committee ko report deti hai. 🏛️
Remedial actions: Decide hotay hain ki kya corrections required hain. 🛠️
Use of Internal Audit Work: 🔗
Audit procedures ka evidence use kar sakte ho apne kaam ke liye (sukoon se, evidence banalo) ✅
Lekin report me reference nahi de sakte internal audit ke kaam ka without permission. ❌
Responsibility hamesha auditor ki hoti hai, even agar internal audit function ka kaam use ho raha ho. ⚖️
Expert ka kaam bhi use kar sakte ho, lekin name report me use nahi hota unless permission ho. 👤
Process Steps: 📝
Internal audit function ke objectives check karo. 🎯
Decide karo kaunsa function aur kaunse log use honge. 👥
Area aur extent define karo (limit of use). 📏
Verify karo kaam evidence ke layak hai ya nahi. ✔️
Approval aur review process follow karo. ✅
Decision Values (3 things): 🎯
Organization status ✅
Relationship status 🤝
Confidence & system metric approach 📊
Objective of Using Internal Audit Function: 🎯
Pehla step: Decide karna ki panel loaded function ya uske log use kar sakte hain ya nahi. ✅
Objective clear hona chahiye before use.
Organization Status & Independence: 🏢
Internal audit function independent nahi hota, company ka part hota hai.
Reporting lines check karni hain: audit committee ko report hoti hai.
Agar internal audit ka function kaam nahi kare, ya bias ho, toh uska use carefully karna hoga. ⚖️
Reporting & Escalation: 📑
Agar errors ya issues report nahi ho rahe, toh audit committee escalate kar sakti hai.
Chairperson issue raise karte hain aur corrective actions ensure karte hain.
Evaluation of Function: 🔍
Organization chart aur policies/procedures check karo.
Conflict of interest ka check zaruri hai.
Objective aur independence evaluate karo.
Qualifications & Resources: 🧑‍💼
Function ke logon ki qualifications: CI, MBA, BBA, etc.
Resource availability: staff aur tools check karo.
Experience aur capability verify karo.
Decision Making: 🛠️
Pehle decide karo ki internal audit function ka kaam use karna hai ya nahi.
Objective, policies, resources, qualifications aur experience check hone ke baad hi kaam assign karo.
Practical Observation: 👀
Agar function properly kaam nahi kar raha (resources, bias, lack of independence), toh use kaam limited scope ke liye karna hoga.
Sabse pehle objective clear hona chahiye, phir hi function ko deploy karo.
Training & Hiring:
Internal audit staff ko regular training di jati hai 🏋️‍♂️.
Hiring ke liye proper tests aur interviews conduct kiye jate hain.
Competency ensure hoti hai taake kam accurate aur effective ho.
Use of Internal Audit Function:
Objective clear hona chahiye ✅.
Systematic design approach ka hona zaruri hai 🛠️.
Reporting lines check karna, conflict of interest na ho 📊.
Use karne se pehle area aur extent define karo.
Evaluation Parameters:
Objective: Clear aur aligned with organizational goals.
Confidence: Staff aur function ki capability ka assessment.
Systematic Approach: Planning, documentation, reporting formats, deadlines, sampling strategy, aur review process.
Decision Making:
Pehle decide karo kya internal audit function ya staff use karna hai ya nahi 📝.
Agar function use karna hai, toh objective, resources, system design aur competency check kar lo.
Audit committee ki approval aur scope define karna zaruri hai 👥.
Assistant ka Use in Internal Audit:
Direct assistant ko use karna allowed hai jab unki competence check ho jaye ✅
Assistant ko function ka scope aur objective samajhna zaruri hai.
Example: Payroll function check – salaries ko employment contracts ke against verify karna 💰
Internal Audit Function:
Function ko use karne se pehle controls test aur validate karo.
Assistant ka kaam karne ke bawajood responsibility auditor ki hi hoti hai ⚖️
Internal audit ke working papers ko review karo pehle rely karne se pehle.
Systematic Approach:
Check karo ke function ke paas structured methodology, documentation, aur timelines hain 📊
Assessment, assignment, aur reporting ke liye step-by-step plan follow karo.
Documentation & Agreements:
Assistant ke objective tests, qualifications, aur experience document karo 👩‍💼👨‍💼
Confidentiality aur internal audit work use karne ki permission ke agreements le lo.
Scope, procedures performed, aur review process record karo.
Review & Verification:
Auditor ko assistant ka kaam bhi review karna zaruri hai.
Sample evidence verify karo pehle rely karne se.
Documentation, conclusions, aur procedures ki compliance confirm karo.
Key Focus Areas:
Organization status, confidence, aur approach.
Critical areas jahan internal audit function ko use ya assign karna hai.
Decision-making rationale for using internal audit function ko document karo.
Audit Committee Involvement:
Scope aur timing management se approve karwana zaruri hai.
Assistant aur internal audit outputs ke use ke clear directions.
Proper supervision, review, aur reporting ensure karo.
