Lecture- 1
📚 Reference: Book Pages -
📚 LECTURE FULL EXPLANATION:
Topic: Written Representation ✍️
🔹 Intro
- Sir ne new topic start kiya: Written Representation.
- Matlab: Auditor client se likhwa leta hai kuch cheezen → confirmation k liye.
- Oral confirmation ≠ Reliable, lekin written confirmation strong evidence hoti hai.
🔑 Logic / Purpose of Written Representation
Oral vs Written
Oral me client kuch bhi keh sakta hai, later deny bhi kar sakta hai.
Written me client zyada careful aur cautious hota hai kyunki baad me usi pe accountability hoti hai.
Standard kehta hai: “Bola ghalat ja sakta hai, likha ghalat nahi ja sakta.” 😅
Example – Related Parties
Client se likhwa lena: “All related party transactions have been recorded and disclosed in FS as per framework.”
Auditor ke paas documentary proof aa jata hai. 📝
Kis se likhwaate hain?
CEO & CFO (top management) → kyunki unke paas business knowledge + FS preparation responsibility hoti hai.
✨ Key Points
Written representation alone evidence nahi hoti, sirf support evidence ke liye use hoti hai.
Auditor apni procedures perform karta hai, phir client se written confirmation leta hai → double check ✅
Management responsibilities:
FS preparation under reporting framework.
Provide all required data/transactions.
Implement internal controls (fraud prevention & detection).
Ensure going concern assumption.
🏷️ Examples Jis cheez ka Written Representation mangwaya jata hai:
All related party transactions disclosed.
FS complete banaye gaye hain under applicable framework.
Internal controls in place.
Fraud prevention & detection responsibility unki hai.
Going concern assessment kiya gaya. 🔍
👉 Wrap-up (Sir ka point):
Written Representation ka purpose ye hai ke auditor ke paas ek formal, careful, documented statement ho management se, jo baad me deny na kar sake. Yeh auditor ke evidence ko support karta hai, replace nahi.
Lecture- 2
📚 Reference: Book Pages -
📚 LECTURE FULL EXPLANATION:
🔹 Management Responsibilities (detail) - FS Preparation → As per reporting framework.
- Internal Controls → Unki zimmedari hai fraud ko prevent & detect karna. 🔐
- Provide Info → Sare records, documents, aur explanations auditor ko dena.
- Going Concern → Yeh assess karna aur ensure karna ke business agle 12 months chal sakta hai. ⏳
🔑 Auditor ka kaam vs Written Representation
Auditor Procedures → Auditor khud testing, verification, aur substantive procedures karega.
Written Representation → Sirf ek additional assurance hai jo auditor ko support karti hai.
Replace nahi karti auditor ki khud ki evidence gathering. 🚫
⚖️ Reliability of Written Representation
Written representation ek evidence hai, but sabse kam reliable hoti hai.
Zyada reliable evidence: External confirmations, independent documents.
Lekin auditor phir bhi leta hai kyunki management ki zimmedari fix karni hoti hai. 📝
✨ Important Notes
Agar management refuse kare written representation dena → red flag 🚨
Auditor ko lagega ke kuch hide ho raha hai.
Is situation me auditor report me modification kar sakta hai.
Written representation hamesha dated hoti hai (audit report ke same date). 📅
Lecture- 3
📚 Reference: Book Pages -
📚 LECTURE FULL EXPLANATION:
🔹 Format of Written Representation - Sir ne bola ke isko Management Representation Letter (MRL) bhi kehte hain. ✉️ - Letter format hota hai, jisme management confirm karti hai apni responsibilities & FS related assertions.
- Addressed hota hai Auditor ko (not to shareholders).
🏷️ Key Contents of MRL
FS preparation under reporting framework.
All information & records provided to auditor.
All transactions properly recorded/disclosed.
Management responsibility for internal controls.
Management responsibility for fraud prevention & detection. 🔐
Going concern assumption ki assessment. ⏳
⚖️ Auditor’s Use of Written Representation
Auditor ke liye yeh ek supporting document hai.
Sir ne emphasize kiya: “Auditor apni evidence gathering chhorr nahi sakta, sirf rep letter pe rely nahi kar sakta.” 🚫
Agar rep letter me contradiction ho auditor ki testing se → auditor further investigation karega. 🔍
👉 Wrap-up (Sir ka point):
MRL = formal document jahan management apne zimmedariyon ka written confirmation deti hai auditor ko. Yeh auditor ke liye ek supporting evidence + legal safeguard hai.
Lecture- 4
📚 Reference: Book Pages -
📚 LECTURE FULL EXPLANATION:
🔹 Standard Reference - Written representation → ISA 580 ke under aata hai. 📖 - Auditor ko yeh zaroor lena parta hai from management.
📌 Assertions usually included in Written Representation
Completeness → Sare records & transactions auditor ko diye gaye. 📂
Accuracy → Information sahi aur material misstatement se free hai. ✅
Existence → Assets & liabilities jo FS me hain wo actually exist karte hain. 🏢
Valuation → Assets & liabilities ka valuation correct hai. 💰
Disclosure → Sare disclosures sahi diye gaye.
⚠️ If Management Refuses Written Representation
Sir ne bola yeh ek serious issue hai. 🚨
Auditor ko lagega ke management kuch hide kar rahi hai.
Result: Auditor disclaim opinion ya qualified opinion de sakta hai (depending on situation).
👉 Wrap-up:
ISA 580 kehtaa hai auditor ko management se ek formal written rep lena hi hai. Agar client refuse kare → iska impact auditor ke report pe directly hota hai.
