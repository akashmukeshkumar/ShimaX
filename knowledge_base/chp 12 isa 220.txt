📚 LECTURE FULL EXPLANATION:
Topic: Quality Control under ISA 220 ✅
1. Overview:
ISA 220 ka main purpose: Maintain high quality in audit 🏆.
Quality maintain karne ka responsibility auditor pe hai 👨‍💼, taake client professional quality services receive kare 🏢✨.
2. Practical Example:
Agar organization ne control lagaya hai ⚙️, jaise sales cycle mein credit limit before selling 💳:
Purpose: avoid bad debts 💸, ensure proper transaction processing 🔄.
Auditor ka role: Check, test aur evaluate controls 🔍🛠️.
Agar controls nahi lagaye: firm non-compliant ho jaati hai with International Standards in Auditing (ISA) ❌.
3. Framework of Quality Control:
Step 1: Control establish karna by the firm 🏢.
Step 2: Control ko assess karna 📊.
Step 3: Control ko test karna 🧪.
Step 4: Audit accordingly 🗂️.
4. Responsibilities:
Engagement Partner (Partner in charge of engagement) 👤:
Ensure quality control ✅.
Verify compliance with ISA and professional standards 📜.
Perform client background investigation 🔎:
Governance check 🏛️
Fraud involvement check 🚨
Senior personnel review 👔
5. Outcome:
Properly maintained quality control ensures:
Accurate audit conclusions 📑✔️
Professional service to client 💼🤝
Compliance with ISA 220 requirements 📘✅
Topic: Client Acceptance & Engagement Partner Responsibilities under Quality Control ✅
1. Client Acceptance & Engagement Letter:
Engagement letter tab tak sign nahi hota jab tak Partner ensure na kare ke client acceptance process complete ho 💌.
Partner pehle sign karega, phir client ko send karega 📤.
Team members ko background investigation karna hota hai 🕵️‍♂️.
Check news, blacklists, fraud cases ❌
Global watch lists / MIS system reports 📊
2. Background Investigation & Documentation:
Purpose: Ensure client aur owners ka integrity check ✅
Documentation: Upload sab details in Canvas system 💻
Include experience of partner, manager, and team members 👥
Ensure proper MIS reporting aur data management 🗂️
3. Partner Responsibilities:
Partner review karta hai engagement team ka kaam 🔍
Ensure compliance with Independence requirements ⚖️
Supervision & guidance: Set direction, review work, approve conclusions 🧭
Decision making includes: Consultation if needed 🗣️
Final sign off by partner before sending letter to client 🖊️
4. Team & Resource Management:
Hiring competent staff with industry knowledge 👨‍💼
Assign tasks & responsibilities based on expertise 🏷️
Supervise work, review conclusions, ensure quality standards met ✅
5. Consultation Process:
High judgment or complex issues require consultation with other partners or experts 👩‍⚖️
Ensures material matters are properly assessed 📑
Outcomes documented for audit trail 📝
6. Quality Control Linkage:
Partner ensures documentation, supervision, consultation, and review are all performed before client acceptance 🏆
Continuous monitoring & review of engagement team performance 📊
Maintain quality control throughout the engagement 🔧

📚 LECTURE FULL EXPLANATION:
Topic: Quality Control under ISA 220 & Partner Review Process ✅
1. Overview:
ISQC (International Standards on Quality Control) aur ISA 220 ka purpose: Ensure high-quality audit performance globally 🌍.
Engagement Partner ko Quality Control Review appoint karna hota hai for every audit engagement 🔍.
2. Appointment of Quality Control Reviewer:
Required for listed companies aur public interest entities 🏦.
Reviewer assess karega engagement team’s work aur ensure karega accuracy aur compliance 📊.
Late appointments possible hain agar one-year lapse ho gaya ho ya delay ho 💡.
3. Role of Quality Control Review:
Review complete hota hai before client acceptance 📜.
Reviewer verify karega:
Work performed correctly ✅
Audit conclusions accurate 📝
Engagement Partner ke views align ho 👥
Documentation: All review actions aur working papers record kiye jaate hain 💻
4. Engagement Partner Responsibilities:
Ensure quality maintained throughout engagement 🏆
Discuss views with Quality Control Reviewer to prevent bias ⚖️
Approve final conclusions based on reviewer feedback 🖊️
Supervision includes direction, guidance, and ensuring proper audit performance 🧭
5. Public Interest Considerations:
Banks ya entities holding public funds considered public interest entities 🏦💰
High public involvement requires stricter quality control aur additional review steps 👀
Reviewer ensures that audit assessment is thorough aur material matters properly evaluated 📑
6. Documentation & Audit Trail:
Saari decisions, reviews, aur consultations document ki jaati hain 📝
Ensure karta hai transparency aur accountability 🔐
Reviewer confirm karta hai ki engagement team ke actions aur conclusions sahi hain aur professional standards follow ho rahe hain ✅
7. Consultation & Complex Judgements:
High judgement cases mein consultation required hoti hai with other partners ya experts 👩‍⚖️
Documentation ensures audit trail for decisions aur assessments 🗂️
Independence maintain hoti hai aur bias in conclusions avoid kiya jata hai ⚖️
Topic: Engagement Quality Control Review & Audit Report Sign-Off ✅
1. Listed Companies & Audit Report:
Pehle listed companies ki process nahi sikhai thi, aj sikhaya ja raha hai 📚
Audit report ko go-ahead sign nahi milta jab tak Quality Control Review complete na ho ✔️
Example: Canvas software me documents attach karte hain for response, control testing aur conclusion 💻
2. Engagement Quality Control (EQC) Process:
EQC ko tick karna hota hai in the system for sign-off ✍️
Sign-off ka matlab: EQC ne document review kiya aur approve kiya ✅
File tab tak close nahi ho sakti jab tak sign-off nahi hota 🚫
3. Engagement Partner Responsibilities:
Engagement partner appoint karta hai EQC partner 🧑‍💼
EQC partner audit judgements ko assess karega, issues discuss karega aur confirmation dega before report sign-off 📝
Ensures objective evaluation aur proper documentation of all conclusions 🗂️
4. EQC Role in Review:
Objective evaluation of audit judgements made by the engagement team 👀
Questions about anomalies, salary, employee counts, interest calculations, provisions etc. 💡
Ensures conclusions drawn in audit report are accurate aur supported by working papers ✅
5. Consultation & Complex Judgements:
EQC may consult third parties if needed 📞
Consultation documented aur decisions traceable 🔍
Maintains independence aur avoids bias between engagement partner aur EQC 👥
6. Control & Monitoring:
Monitoring process ensures proper audit execution aur independent checks 🛡️
Audit Committee monitors internal audit department reports 📑
Ensures system aur control processes properly operate even after initial review ⚙️
