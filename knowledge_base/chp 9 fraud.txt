📚 LECTURE FULL EXPLANATION:
Main Concept:
Fraud chapter (ISA 240) ko practical way me samjhaya ja raha hai 📚. Auditor ka main objective hota hai reasonable assurance hasil karna ke financial statements material misstatement se free hon — chahe wo error ki wajah se ho ya fraud ki wajah se ✅.
Key Points from Lecture:
Material Misstatement:
Ho sakta hai error ki wajah se ⚠️.
Ho sakta hai fraud ki wajah se 🚨.
Fraud Risk Assessment:
Fraud ka chance wahan hota hai jahan opportunity ho 🔍.
Opportunity tab milti hai jab controls kamzor ya absent hon ❌.
Examples (Realistic Cases):
Inventory / Petty Cash without Physical Access Controls:
Agar physical access control nahi hai → koi bhi inventory ya petty cash chura sakta hai 💸.
Risk: Asset misappropriation → FS me misstatement 📊.
Mobile Manufacturing Warehouse:
Open access sab ko → fraud risk high 📦.
Petty Cash without Heavy Lock Box:
Har kisi ko access dena = theft ka strong risk 🔑.
Control Weakness & Fraud Risk:
Control absent → opportunity milti hai → fraud commit ho sakta hai 🚫.
Auditor ko risk identify karte waqt sochna hoga: “Kis FS line item me fraud ka possibility zyada hai?” 🤔
Fraud ki Language Me:
Asset ka disappearance (e.g., inventory chori) = Misappropriation of assets 🛑.
Important Lines (Highlighted):
“Fraud wahan hota hai jahan opportunity ho.” 🔍
“Control absent ho to fraud ka risk barh jata hai.” ⚠️
“Asset misappropriation ek common fraud type hai jo FS me misstatement la sakta hai.” 📊
Examples in Lecture:
🔍 Fraud Indicators & Red Flags
Fraud inventory aur cash me bhi ho sakta hai — jaise daily records na banana ya manipulate karna.
Unexpected situations & changes (financial data me sudden variation) fraud ka early signal ho sakta hai ⚠️.
Example: Provision for bad debts me ajeeb aur sudden changes.
📊 Example – Bad Debts Provision
Pichle saal provision 15% tha → iss saal achanak 25% kar diya.
Aisi sudden & unjustified change in estimate → fraud ka suspicion 😮.
Management ne estimates alter kiye taake profit manipulate ho sake.
Investigation me pata chala:
CEO, CFO aur poori management chain involve thi.
Profit inflate karne ke liye accounting estimates ka misuse hua.
Result: massive write-off (balance sheet ka ~60%).
💡 Financial Reporting Fraud
Fraud sirf cash chori nahi hota, financial statements ko manipulate karna bhi fraud hai.
Example: Assets ki useful life jaan bujh ke barha dena taake depreciation kam aur profit zyada dikh sake 💹.
🛠 Audit Procedures – Estimates & Useful Life Check
Auditor ko historical financial data aur prior year estimates compare karne chahiye.
Management ke estimates ko reasonableness test dena zaroori hai.
Procedures:
Previous year vs current year comparison.
Receivables/advances ka aging schedule check karna.
Useful life changes ke liye documentary proof lena.
Replacement cycle ke baare me inquiries karna.
Depreciation calculations recalculate & verify karna.
🔍 Fraud Risk in Estimates & Judgements
Financial reporting me management estimates aur judgements ka misuse fraud ka major source hota hai.
Auditor ko pata hona chahiye ke kaunse areas high estimation risk wale hote hain — e.g., impairment, provisions, fair value measurements.
Example: Depreciation method ya discount rate ko manipulate karke profit impact karna.
📊 Real-Life Example – Asset Valuation
Company ne goodwill impairment test ke assumptions inflate kiye.
Discount rate artificially kam dikhaya taake asset value high lage.
Result: Overstated assets aur misled investors 😮.
🛠 Auditor’s Response to Estimation Fraud Risk
Professional skepticism maintain karna.
Independent expectation develop karna aur compare karna management ke estimates se.
Significant assumptions verify karna — market data ya independent expert se.
Analytical procedures apply karna — historical trends vs current year.
Example: Agar bad debts provision sudden gir gaya without reason → detailed inquiry.
📋 Common Areas of Fraud in Estimates
Provision for bad debts 📉
Warranty provisions 🛠
Asset useful life 🏢💻
Fair value of investments 📈
Impairment tests 💡
🔍 Fraud Risk in Revenue Recognition
Revenue fraud bohot common hota hai kyunke yeh directly profit impact karta hai 💰.
Management pressure hota hai targets meet karne ka → leads to early, fake, ya inflated revenue recognition.
Example: Sales ko record kar dena jab goods abhi deliver nahi huay.
📊 Real-Life Example – Fake Sales
Company ne year-end pe large fake invoices banaye.
Goods deliver nahi hue but revenue record kar liya.
Result: Financial statements inflated aur investors ko mislead kiya 😮.
🛠 Auditor’s Response to Revenue Fraud Risk
Cut-off testing: Ensure sales correct accounting period me record hui hain 📅.
Customer confirmations lena.
Large / unusual transactions ka review karna, specially year-end pe.
Supporting documents verify karna (delivery notes, contracts).
Example: Agar December me bohot unusual sales hui → investigate.
Lecture- 2

📚 LECTURE FULL EXPLANATION:
Core Concepts 📝
Fraud detect karna: Har unusual situation ka matlab fraud nahi hota — context samajhna zaroori hai 😉.
Bank wali example: Agar senior management unrealistic targets set kare → financial reporting manipulate hone ka chance.
Misappropriation of assets: Company ke assets ya cash chura lena 💰.
Internal controls:
Procurement me agar supplier ka relation nikle (friend, family) → conflict of interest ka risk.
Weak controls → fraud ka mauka.
Objective: Identify karna ke kaun se situations fraud risk dikhate hain.
IAS 315: Normal aur unusual situations ka difference samjhata hai.
Fraud risk ke factors:
Opportunity (resources ka access).
Pressure (personal ya financial stress).
Intent (deliberate act).
Key Risk Indicators ⚠️
Senior management ka sudden resign bina reason → suspicious situation.
Accounting estimates ka sudden change (e.g., bad debts 15% se 2% ho jana) → manipulation ka doubt.
New software / ERP ka implementation:
Data transfer me error ka risk.
Fraud tabhi jab intentional manipulation ho.
Finance/accounts staff ka high turnover → segregation of duties disturb ho sakta hai.
New employees process knowledge ke bina → unintentional error ka chance.
Other Points 💡
Har error fraud nahi hota — fraud ke liye intention hona chahiye.
Audit software ka use:
Random sampling → unbiased selection.
Sample size software decide karega, auditor ka control nahi hota.
Data migration risk:
System change par accuracy & completeness check karna.
Fraud tabhi jab numbers intentionally alter kiye gaye ho.
Examples 📌
Core Concepts 📝
Fraudulent Financial Reporting: Jab management ya employees intentionally financial statements manipulate karte hain to mislead stakeholders.
Common Methods:
Revenue inflate karna.
Expenses kam dikhana.
Assets overstate karna.
Misappropriation of Assets: Company ke assets chura lena ya misuse karna.
Difference:
Fraudulent financial reporting → FS ka galat presentation.
Misappropriation → physical assets ka loss.
Auditor ka role: Fraud detect karna primary responsibility nahi, lekin auditor ko reasonable assurance lena hota hai ke FS material misstatement free ho.
Fraud Risk Factors ⚠️
Management override of controls:
Journal entries ka manipulation.
Accounting policies ka inappropriate change.
Incentives/pressures:
Bonus targets achieve karne ka pressure.
Market expectations meet karna.
Opportunities:
Weak internal controls.
Related party transactions ka misuse.
Attitudes/rationalizations:
“Sab karte hain” type mindset.
Red Flags 🚩
Last minute journal entries ka large volume.
Unusual adjustments at year-end.
Related party transactions without disclosure.
Consistently meeting or just beating forecasts.
Auditor’s Response 🕵️‍♂️
Professional skepticism maintain karna.
Journal entries test karna (especially unusual ones).
Management estimates critically assess karna.
Related party transactions ka proper disclosure verify karna.
Analytical procedures use karna to spot anomalies.
Examples 📌
Key Discussion Points 💡
Fraud triangle recap:
Opportunity → Weak internal controls aur override chances.
Pressure → Personal ya business related.
Rationalization/Incentives → Employee ya management justification.
Team engagement & discussion:
Team ko involve kar ke ideas exchange karo.
Individual statements aur team discussions document karo.
Focus on identifying kahan aur kaise fraud ho sakta hai.
Documentation importance:
Har risk assessment ka detailed record rakho.
Discussions aur examples ka proper documentation ensure karo.
Analytical review:
FS review ke dauran unusual trends aur spikes detect karo.
Revenue recognition, year-end adjustments aur micro-level analysis pe focus karo.
Risk Indicators ⚠️
Lifestyle mismatch: Employee ka lifestyle unke declared income se zyada ho.
Complex transactions: Jo clearly business purpose justify nahi karti.
Frequent accounting policy changes: Bina solid reason ke.
Unusual sales timing: Jaise December mein abnormal sales bookings.
New customer approvals: Rapid onboarding ya approvals without proper authorization.
Team Guidance 👥
Always engage team before drawing conclusions.
Focus on how fraud can occur, not just what numbers show.
Verify every suspicious transaction, document evidence.
Use professional judgment but avoid assumptions without support.
Lecture- 3
📚 Reference: Book Pages -
📚 LECTURE FULL EXPLANATION:
Management Override of Control
Management apne banaye hue rules ya controls ko khud override kar sakti hai.
Sirf theoretical checks ya responsible control dekhne se fraud miss ho sakta hai.
Detecting Misstatements
Misstatement detect karna theory aur practical dono mein essential hai.
External audit mein errors detect karne ke chances zyada hote hain.
Internal Control Review
Internal controls ka review karna zaruri hai.
Agar controls absent hon, fraud indicators bhi miss ho sakte hain. ⚠️
Fraud Triangle
Opportunity: Individual ke paas fraud karne ka chance.
Incentive/Reward: Personal ya professional benefit ka possibility.
Pressure: External ya internal pressure jo individual ko fraud karne pe majboor kare.
Practical Examples
Loan & Finance Case: Company ko loan chahiye aur finance team ko project assign kiya jata hai; agar management ke paas opportunity aur benefit hai, fraud ho sakta hai.
Physical Access & Controls: Weak physical access control se fraud ke chances barh jate hain.
Audit Procedures
Market prices aur external confirmations verify karna zaruri hai. 📝
Third-party confirmations aur balance verification fraud detect karne mein madadgar hote hain.
Internal Incentives & Pressure
Salary, bonus, promotion ya targets ki wajah se pressure ya incentive fraud trigger kar sakta hai.
Team Discussions
Team ke sath discuss karna important hai ke fraud kahan aur kaise ho sakta hai.
Debt Covenants & Liabilities
Management kabhi current liabilities intentionally kam dikhata hai taki profitability achi lage.
Aise areas mein bhi fraud ka potential ho sakta hai.
Potential Fraud Areas ⚠️
Current liabilities, revenue recognition aur classification changes se fraud ho sakta hai. 💰
Provision adjustments aur book entries ke peeche motives ho sakte hain. 📊
Team Discussions & Assessment 👥
Team ko engage karke potential fraud areas identify karna zaruri hai. 🔍
Internal discussions se samajh aata hai ke management ke decisions aur past events kahan impact karte hain. 🗂️
Sales & Management Influence 📈
Sales manager ke targets achieve karne ke liye opportunistic actions le sakta hai. 🎯
Control mechanisms jaise GD entry verification se fraud prevent hota hai. 🛡️
High-level individuals ke paas override ya collusion ke chances ho sakte hain. ⚡
Internal Control Insights 🏢
Internal audit se pata chalta hai ke sales orders aur bookings properly recorded hain ya nahi. 📑
Proper control design se risk minimize hota hai, jaise credit approvals aur payroll system ke controls. ✅
Inquiry & Preparation ✍️
Management se inquiries ke liye proper preparation zaruri hai. 📝
Pre-assessment aur understanding of past adjustments help karti hai accurate evaluation mein. 📌
Observation & Verification 👀
External confirmations aur physical access controls verify karna important hai. 🔒
Errors aur unusual transactions ko trace karna audit ka essential part hai. 🕵️
Integrity & Honest Practices 💎
Management ke honest aur integrity-based actions fraud probability kam karte hain. 👍
Auditor ko preconceptions aur bias se bachna chahiye; observations factual honi chahiye. ⚖️
Practical Implementation 🛠️
Audit team ko clear understanding honi chahiye ke potential fraud kahan aur kaise ho sakta hai. 🔄
Systematic approach: discussions → risk identification → control verification → inquiry → documentation. 📚
Audit Reporting & Fraud Identification 📝
Auditor ka kaam: identify karna aur report karna potential fraud areas.
Focus: ensure karo ke fraud ka evidence accurately capture ho aur report me include ho.
Observation: check karo management ne kya controls set kiye aur unka documentation sahi hai ya nahi.
Role of Assistants & Departments 👥
Assistants ka role: analytical procedures aur regular reviews perform karna.
Departments ka involvement: cross-verification aur justification provide karna.
Frequency: monthly ya periodic checks, taake discipline aur risk minimize ho.
Control Design & Assessment ⚙️
Internal control design aur uski effectiveness assess karo.
Nature & extent of control: kaunse processes cover ho rahe hain, kaunse areas test ho rahe hain.
High-risk areas pe focus: cash handling, credit approvals, payroll, etc.
Inquiry Process & Management Interaction 🗣️
Direct management se interaction: past events aur transaction discrepancies ke bare me inquire karo.
Objective: validate karo fraud assessment aur evaluate karo real exposure.
Ensure karo ke management responses documented ho future reference ke liye.
Transaction Testing & Verification 💳
Transaction-level analysis: verify account balances aur related party transactions.
Example: provision for bad debts, revenue recognition, cash vs bank discrepancies.
Goal: identify karo potential misstatements ya intentional manipulations.
Governance & Oversight Structure 🏛️
Governance framework aur accountability lines samjho.
Role of audit committee: monitor audit results aur ensure karo ke management responsible hai.
No tolerance approach: deliberate misreporting ko penalize karo taake control environment maintain ho.
Risk & Control Communication 📢
Communicate karo control design, gaps, aur potential exposure relevant stakeholders ko.
Ensure karo ke sab departments aware ho responsibilities ke bare me taake fraud prevent ho.
Use anonymous reporting mechanisms for sensitive information.
Practical Takeaways ✅
Document karo sab observations, assessments, aur management responses.
Maintain logical process flow: observation → inquiry → assessment → documentation.
Evaluate control environment: extent, frequency, aur integrity of processes.
Identify high-risk areas aur plan focused audit procedures accordingly.

Lecture- 5
📚 Reference: Book Pages -
📚 LECTURE FULL EXPLANATION:
1. Revenue Recognition Risks 💡
Revenue ko manipulate kiya ja sakta hai even agar competition ho.
Private limited companies usually fraud nahi kar sakti agar IPO plan ho raha ho. ✅
Hamesha unusual entries ya missing customer info check karo.
Agar customer info (address, identity) missing hai → transaction exist nahi karta → record nahi karna chahiye. ⚠️
2. Management & Internal Controls
Management kabhi bhi light of controls override kar sakti hai → misstatement kahin bhi ho sakti hai.
Internal controls foolproof nahi hain; hamesha exceptions test karo.
Control failure se audit me scope limitation ho sakta hai. ❌
3. Practical Audit Checks ⏰
Unusual entries verify karo jo normal hours ke bahar post hui ho (weekends, after office).
Case Example: PTCL audit – millions of customer revenue entries manipulate ho sakti hain even controls ke hone ke bawajood.
Har entry trace karo: Debit vs Credit, GL codes ke against verify karo (e.g., 1026 vs 1024).
4. System & Accounting Relationships
Automatic postings: interest calculate hota hai pre-set code & rate ke basis par.
System setup, GL relationship aur control mechanism samajhna zaroori hai accurate audit ke liye.
5. Sampling vs Targeted Testing 🎯
Sirf samples se errors ya fraud detect nahi hota.
Targeted testing karo for unusual ya high-risk entries.
Unusual entries zaroori nahi ke fraud ho, lekin suspicion create kar sakti hain.
Related entries extract karo, customer aur contract ke against map karo, postings verify karo.
6. Key Observations
Control failure hone par auditors ko alternative procedures use karni padti hain.
Management override hamesha risk hai.
1. Manufacturing Audit Insights 🏭
Manufacturing me bhi audit apply hota hai, processes same concept pe based hoti hain.
System me entries ki knowledge lena zaroori hai, actual transactions track karna important hai.
Ek entry se multiple GL numbers ho sakte hain → har GL number track karna chahiye.
Debit aur credit accounts ka verification aur GL numbers ka match karna essential hai.
2. Testing & Verification 🔍
Pure saal ki entries test karna practical nahi → targeted entries test karo.
Suspicious ya unusual entries identify karna primary task hai.
Example: December month ke entries zyada risk carry karti hain, closing balances ke wajah se.
Closing balances aur supporting documents ko match karna zaroori hai.
3. Entry Ownership & Control 👤
Revenue, provision, aur expense entries alag log create karte hain → segregation of duties maintain hona chahiye.
Check karo kisne kaunsi entry post ki hai aur system me kiska access hai.
Passwords aur login secure hone chahiye → physical & system access control critical hai.
4. System Mapping & GL Analysis 🖥️
Revenue entries ka GL ke against mapping check karo.
GL ledger aur filters use karke identify karo kaunse assistant ya staff ne post kiya.
Jo exercises ya sample data diye gaye hain unko bhi verify karna chahiye.
Automated postings aur mapping changes ko track karna zaroori hai.
5. Control Testing & Limitations ⚖️
Detective controls monitor karte hain unusual activities ko.
Staff ko allowed permissions se beyond entry create nahi karna chahiye.
Agar control fail hota hai → scope limitation consider karna zaroori hai.
Audit ka focus: ensure ke sirf authorized log hi entries create karte hain aur activities traceable hain.
1. Entry Verification & Documentation 🗂️
Sab entries kisne banai, yeh track karna zaroori hai → system ke through verify karo.
Independent manual edits possible nahi → har edit traceable hona chahiye.
Documentation check karo: supporting documents aur GL numbers verify karne hain.
System-generated aur manually created entries ka clear distinction maintain karo.
Jo bhi unusual ya suspicious entries ho, unko highlight karo for further review.
2. System-Generated Entries & Sampling ⚙️
System generator multiple entries create karta hai → stress lene ki zarurat nahi.
Large dataset ke liye sampling approach adopt karo.
Example: PTCL ya revenue percentage calculations ke liye random sample select kar ke verify karo.
Automated postings aur mapping changes ko bhi track karo.
Agar 500–600 entries ek hi area me aaye → tension nahi, system consistently generate karta hai.
3. Round Numbers & Red Flags 🔴
Round number entries suspicious ho sakti hain → indicate karta hai possible manual adjustment.
Example: Bank audits, electricity bills: 25,000, 5 lakh, 10 million.
Round numbers ke peeche ka reason trace karna zaroori hai.
Agar repeatedly appear ho rahe hain → manual intervention ya fraud ka indication.
Round numbers ko context ke sath verify karo, ensure ke logically justify ho.
4. Description & Narrative Check 📝
Description field me correct details aur original reason match hona chahiye.
Example: Maintenance ledger → vendor aur purpose check karo.
Agar description vague ya incorrect → flag for review.
Multiple entries cross-check karo for consistency.
Random descriptions ka pattern observe karo → unnatural ya suspicious patterns note karo.
5. Professional & Legal Duty ⚖️
Auditor ka professional aur legal duty hai ke fraud identify kare aur report kare.
High-level supervision aur experienced team members include karo for subtle fraud detection.
Proper documentation aur evidence maintain karo → complete audit trail.
Agar entries incorrect ya fraud detect ho → report to management immediately.
6. Additional Audit Insights 🔍
Independent verification ensures proper traceability.
GL mapping aur relational analysis zaroori hai to detect unusual entries.
Sampling aur targeted testing efficient hai large data sets me.
Ensure ke sirf authorized log hi entries create karte hain.
Round numbers aur unusual descriptions ko specially monitor karo.
Agar professional/legal duty ke context me required ho → report proactively.
