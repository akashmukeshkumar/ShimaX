Lecture- 1
📚 Reference: Book Pages -
📚 LECTURE FULL EXPLANATION:
🔹 Basic Concept
Subsequent events (SE) ka matlab: FS year-end (reporting date) ke baad aur auditor’s report sign hone ke beech me jo events hote hain.
Example: FS date = 31 Dec 2022, Audit report sign = March 2023 → jo bhi events iss duration me huay = Subsequent Events.
👉 Auditor ko yeh check karna hai:
Kya event adjust hona chahiye FS me?
Kya event disclose hona chahiye FS me?
🔹 Objective of ISA 560
Auditor ki responsibility define karta hai:
Year-end ke baad → report date tak hone wale events ka assessment.
Dekhna: adjustment/disclosure required hai ya nahi.
Agar auditor ko report date ke baad koi material fact pata chale → uska response bhi ISA 560 me cover hota hai.
🔹 Definition
Subsequent Events = Events between FS date & auditor’s report date.
Auditor ka focus → Evidence obtain karna ke:
Adjustment required tha ya nahi.
Disclosure required tha ya nahi.
🔹 Adjusting vs Non-Adjusting Events
Adjusting Events
Wo events jinki conditions (fs ana ha)reporting date par exist karti thi.
Matlab: Issue pehle se chal raha tha, bas confirm baad me hua.
Example: 25 Dec 2022 ko accident hua, case file 25 Feb 2023 me hua. Condition (accident) 31 Dec 2022 ko exist karti thi → Adjusting Event.
Non-Adjusting Events
Wo events jo (fs ana ha)reporting date ke baad originate hote hain.
Matlab: 31 Dec 2022 tak condition exist nahi karti thi.
Example: 25 Feb 2023 ko hi accident hua → 31 Dec 2022 pe koi evidence nahi tha → Non-Adjusting Event.
🔹 Why Important?
Auditor ka role = Ensure kare:
Adjusting events → FS me incorporate ho.
Non-adjusting events → Agar material ho to disclose kare.
Reporting me clarity ho ke responsibility auditor ki hi hai.
✨ Quick Flow
FS close → Events occur → Auditor evaluate:
Condition existed @ FS date? ✅ Adjusting.
Condition did NOT exist @ FS date? ❌ Non-adjusting.
⚖️ Case Discussion
Case file hua → Lawyer list delete/confirmation → Ek aur case aya.
Disclosure LED: agar mujhe info milti hai → main record kar leta hu.
Agar mere lawyer bolta hai ke chances remote hain → “Do Nothing”.
Agar lawyer bolta hai possible hai → sirf Disclosure.
Agar probable hai → Provision record karni hogi.
📝 Key Words yaad rakhna:
Unlikely → Expense record nahi hoga.
Possible → Disclosure only.
Remote → Do Nothing.
📅 Event after Reporting Date
Example: 31 Dec 2022 ke baad customer bankrupt ho gaya.
Lekin bankruptcy ki condition already exist karti thi → Adjusting Event.
Agar sirf baad me fire/accident hua → 31 Dec par condition exist nahi karti thi → Non-Adjusting Event.
👉 Adjusting = Condition exist karti ho reporting date pe. 👉 Non-Adjusting = Condition baad me hui.
🔥 Example: Factory Fire
31 Dec tak company financially strong thi.
Jan me factory me fire → bankruptcy ho gayi.
Non-Adjusting Event ❌
Sirf Disclosure dena hoga agar material ho.
📦 Inventory Example
Inventory cost = Rs.100.
Net realizable value (NRV) = Rs.95.
Kyun? → Competitor ne new product launch kar diya.
Matlab inventory reporting date pe hi impaired thi.
Adjusting Event → Inventory ko 95 pe record karo.
🧾 Rule: Inventory hamesha Lower of Cost or NRV pe record hoti hai.
🌊 Flood Example
Inventory cost = 100.
Flood ke baad inventory sirf 90 pe bikhi.
Reporting date pe flood nahi tha → inventory thik thi.
Non-Adjusting Event.
Loss ko next year (2023 FS) me recognize karo.
🛠️ Warranty Provision Example
28 Dec 2022 ko sales hui → warranty di 1 month ke liye.
Estimate: 20 per unit repair cost, 1000 units.
40 units return → provision banayi Rs.800.
Baad me pata chala returns zyada hain (100 units).
Provision update karni hogi → Adjusting Event.
Kyunki obligation 2022 me create hui thi.
👉 Provision update karna zaroori hai if new info mil jaye before audit report date.

📚 LECTURE FULL EXPLANATION:
Aaj ka Topic: Auditor ko kaun si audit procedures perform karni chahiye to identify subsequent events? 🤔
1. Understanding Management’s Process 📝
Auditor pehle samjhta hai k management ne kaun se controls/procedures banaye hain SE identify karne k liye.
Example: Agar year-end ke baad lawsuit file hota hai → legal counsel finance dept ko inform karta hai → finance assess karta hai adjusting/non-adjusting.
Isi tarah board/audit committee ki meetings ke minutes finance ko forward kiye jate hain.
Auditor inhi processes se info gather karta hai.
2. Inquiries of Management and Others 🙋‍♂️
Auditor management aur relevant logon se poochta hai (kabhi kabhi external legal counsel se bhi): - Koi nayi commitment, borrowing, guarantee, contract? - Koi natural disaster (fire, flood, earthquake) jisse assets affect huay? - Pending litigation/legal claims ka development? - Significant acquisition, disposal, merger, investment? - Share capital issue/instrument issue? - Koi event jo going concern pe doubt dalta ho? - Provisions/estimates me koi change (jaise warranty provisions) due to new info? - Recoverability issue (major customer bankrupt hone k baad)?
3. Reading Minutes of Meetings 📑
Auditor board of directors, audit committee aur doosri key meetings ke minutes read karta hai.
In se auditor ko info milti hai: customers, contracts, financial issues ya going concern se related.
4. Reviewing Interim Financial Information 📈
Auditor interim FS check karta hai jo year-end k baad banayi gayi ho.
Budgets, forecasts aur cash flow projections bhi review karta hai → liquidity ya going concern risk identify karne k liye.
5. Review of Subsequent Transactions 💳
Auditor year-end ke baad huay major transactions (capital expenditure, financing arrangements, loan repayment) check karta hai → dekhne k liye k kya yeh reporting date ki condition ko indicate karte hain.
6. External Legal Counsel Confirmation ⚖️
Auditor kabhi kabhi external legal counsel se confirm bhi karta hai litigation/claims ke bare me jo year-end ke baad file huay.
7. Written Representations from Management ✍️
Auditor written representation leta hai from management jisme confirm ho k sab SE jo adjustment/disclosure require karte hain → unko auditor ko communicate kiya gaya hai.

Facts Discovered After Auditor’s Report Date but Before FS Issue to Public
Kabhi kabhi auditor ko report date ke baad aur FS ke issue hone se pehle koi new fact milta hai.
Agar woh fact auditor ko report date par pata hota, toh usne apni report amend karni hoti.
Yeh situation tricky hoti hai → auditor ko dobara management aur TCWG se discuss karna parta hai.
Case Example:
Report date: 2 March 2023
Report sign kar di gayi, lekin FS abhi market/public ko issue nahi hui.
8 March 2023 ko pata chala: ek major customer bankrupt ho gaya hai.
Auditor assess karega: kya yeh adjusting event hai? Agar haan, toh FS me amendment zaroori hai.
Auditor’s Response Process:
Discuss with Management / TCWG 🤝
Auditor pehle yeh fact management aur governance walon ke saath discuss karega.
Determine karega: adjusting hai ya non-adjusting event?
If Management Agrees to Amend FS: ✍️
Auditor amended FS ko check karega → kya amendments sahi ki gayi hain?
Example: Receivables 100m the, customer bankrupt hua aur ab sirf 20m recoverable hai → provision 80m ka banana hoga.
Auditor ensure karega ki adjustment proper hai.
Auditor new amended audit report issue karega (with new date e.g., 9 March 2023).
Important: Auditor ko phir se subsequent events procedures perform karne honge new report date tak.
If Management Refuses to Amend FS: 🚫
Yeh case next me discuss hoga (exception concept).
Scenario Recap: Agar audit report sign hone ke baad auditor ko koi fact/error pata lagta hai jo agar pehle pata hota toh financial statements amend karne parte, toh auditor kya karega?
🔹 Case 1: Normal Amendment
Management agree karti hai ke puray financial statements (FS) amend kare.
Is case me auditor:
Apne extended procedures perform karega dobara.
Governance se approval lega.
Puray FS dobara issue honge, puray audit procedures dobara perform honge.
Auditor new audit report issue karega (puri nayi report with new date).
🔹 Case 2: Exception Allowed (Dual Dating)
Lekin agar Financial Reporting Framework aur Law/Regulation allow karta hai ke:
Management sirf specific amendment kare (full FS re-issue na kare).
Governance approval bhi sirf usi amendment tak limited ho.
Toh auditor ko bhi ye chhutti milti hai ke wo:
Sirf usi amendment par audit procedures perform kare.
Puray FS ko dobara check na kare.
👉 Yahan auditor Dual Dating Concept apply karega.
📝 Dual Dating Concept Explained:
Auditor apni audit report me 2 dates likhta hai:
Original Audit Report Date → Jis din tak auditor ne puray FS par audit procedures perform kiye.
New (Amendment) Date → Sirf us amendment par jo baad me add/disclose hui hai.
Example wording:
“Except as to Note Y (related to subsequent event), our audit procedures are completed as of [New Date].”
📌 Matlab:
Original audit ka kaam dobara perform nahi kiya gaya.
Sirf amendment related procedures perform kiye gaye.
🔍 Summary:
Agar management puray FS amend kare → Auditor new report issue karega (no dual dating).
Agar framework/law allow karta hai sirf ek amendment → Auditor dual dating use karega.
Report me do dates aayengi:
Original date = puray FS ke liye.
New date = sirf amendment ke liye.

📃 Scenario: Management Refuses to Amend
Ab tak humne dekha tha agar management agree kar le FS amend karne par toh auditor nayi report issue karega.
Lekin agar management refuse kar de aur bole ke hum FS ko amend nahi karenge?
Is case me auditor ka next step depend karega situation par.
1. Report Signed but Not Yet Issued ✍️
Agar auditor ne apni audit report sign kar di hai lekin abhi tak wo management ya public ko issue nahi ki gayi → auditor apni report modify karke nayi report issue kar sakta hai.
Example: Opinion ko modify karega (Qualified / Adverse) depending on materiality.
Matlab: Koi issue nahi, kyunki report abhi tak issue hi nahi hui.
2. Report Already Issued but FS Not Issued to Public 📤
Agar audit report management ko di ja chuki hai, lekin FS abhi market/public ko issue nahi hui:
Auditor management ko notify karega ke: “Please FS ko amend karo ya phir abhi kisi third party ko issue mat karo.”
Auditor governance walon ko bhi inform karega (TCWG).
Agar management phir bhi refuse kare aur FS ko public me issue kar de → auditor further action lega (jaise ke regulators ya AGM me matter disclose karna).
3. Report & FS Already Issued to Public 🌍
Agar auditor ki report aur FS dono public me chali gayi hain, aur baad me auditor ko pata chalta hai ke ek material fact galat ya missing tha:
Auditor pehle management aur governance ko inform karega.
Request karega ke corrected FS issue kiye jayein.
Agar wo refuse karein → auditor external actions lega:
AGM me disclose karega.
Regulators (e.g., SECP, ICAP, SBP) ko notify karega.
Ensure karega ke users ko aware kar diya jaye ke purane FS reliable nahi hain.
📌 Summary:
Report signed but not issued → Modify karke nayi report do.
Report issued, FS not public → Notify management & TCWG, stop FS issuance.
Report & FS issued public → Inform management, regulators, aur public (AGM/disclosure).
👉 Always remember: Auditor’s role = Protect FS users from relying on wrong/misleading information.
🔎 Facts Discovered After FS Issue to Public
Ab situation change ho gayi hai: ab Financial Statements (FS) market/public ko issue ho chuki hain.
Auditor ko ek new fact/error milta hai jo agar pehle pata hota toh report modify karni parti.
Is case me process thoda tough ho jata hai kyunki ab logon ke paas FS aur report dono ja chuke hote hain.
Auditor’s Steps:
Discuss with Management & TCWG 🤝
Pehle auditor management aur governance (TCWG) ko situation batayega.
Assess karega: kya amendment FS me required hai?
If Management Agrees to Amend FS: ✍️
Auditor amendment verify karega (provision/disclosure etc. sahi hua ya nahi).
Auditor extended subsequent events procedures perform karega new report date tak.
Important: Kyunki FS market me issue ho chuki thi, ab management ka kaam hai jinko pehle FS diye gaye the unko notify karna.
Matlab management notice bhejti hai + amended FS + new auditor report.
Auditor ensure karega ke ye communication proper tariqe se ho.
2 Extra Responsibilities:
Check karein ke amendment proper hai.
Ensure karein ke previously issued FS users ko inform kiya gaya hai.
If Management Refuses to Amend FS: 🚫
Auditor unko formally notify karega ke yeh fact material hai.
Agar phir bhi management action nahi leti, toh auditor ka duty banta hai ke apni report pe reliance prevent kare.
Yeh depend karega law/regulation pe (options may include: regulator ko report karna, shareholders ko notify karna, public notice, etc.).
🔹 Exception Allowed (Dual Dating / Limited Amendment)
Agar law/regulation allow karta hai ke sirf limited amendment ho (full FS re-issue na ho):
Management sirf relevant disclosure amend karegi.
Auditor sirf usi amendment pe audit procedures perform karega.
Auditor report me dual dating use karega:
1st Date = Original audit report date (puray FS ke liye).
2nd Date = Sirf amendment/disclosure ke liye.
Auditor ya toh:
Emphasis of Matter (EoM) / Other Matter (OM) para add kare FS note ka reference dete hue, ya
New report issue kare with explanation.
🔍 Summary:
Agar FS public ko issue nahi hui thi: sirf amendment aur new report (ya dual dating).
Agar FS public ko issue ho chuki thi:
Management ne amend kiya toh:
Amendment verify karo.
Ensure karo previous users ko inform kiya gaya hai.
Management ne refuse kiya toh:
Auditor ka duty = prevent reliance on report (through legal/regulatory actions).
