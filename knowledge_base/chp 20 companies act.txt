📚 LECTURE FULL EXPLANATION:
🔹 Topic: Companies Act ka audit perspective – focus on auditor appointment & tenure.
1️⃣ Class Focus:
Company law ka complete coverage nahi, sirf auditor-related provisions cover karein.
Background: Kuch provisions course mein add kiye gaye hain jisse auditor responsibilities clear ho.
2️⃣ First Auditor Appointment:
Jab company incorporate hoti hai, pehla auditor appoint hota hai.
Certificate of Incorporation milne ke baad auditor appoint hota hai.
Responsibility: Pehla auditor appoint karna company ka kaam hai, auditor ka nahi.
Board of Directors appoint karte hain.
3️⃣ Tenure of First Auditor:
Pehla auditor apni position First Annual General Meeting (AGM) tak hold karega.
AGM ki deadline 16 months incorporation ke baad (agar pehle na ho) ✅
Agar pehla auditor pehle saal mein audit nahi karta, responsibility next year ki hoti hai.
4️⃣ Subsequent Auditor Appointment:
Next auditor ya same EY (existing) ho sakta hai.
Auditor ko management recommend karte hain, final decision shareholders ke haath mein.
Governance check important: auditors ka selection, unka recommendation aur shareholder approval.
5️⃣ Auditor Change Process:
Agar auditor change karna hai, SECP ko inform karna hoga along with reasons.
Reasons can include: tenure completion, family issues, withdrawal, etc.
Notices AGM se 7 days pehle jaate hain.
Shareholders with ≥10% voting rights bhi propose kar sakte hain.
6️⃣ Communication & Representation:
Auditor ko change ki notice 2 days pehle bhejna mandatory hai.
AGM mein auditor ka representation read kiya jata hai: koi objection hai ya nahi.
🔹 Topic: Auditor ki appointment, consent, aur casual vacancy ka process.
1️⃣ Appointment Timing & Tenure:
Pehle auditor ka tenure khatam hone ke baad, next auditor (same EY ya new) appoint karna zaruri hai.
Auditor ki appointment Board recommendation ke basis pe hoti hai aur auditor se consult kar ke hoti hai.
Auditor ka consent mandatory hai. Consent na ho → appointment invalid.
2️⃣ Consent (Consultation) Process:
Retiring auditor apna consent dega reappointment ke liye.
Members/shareholders ko auditor change ke bare me inform karna zaruri hai.
7 din pehle notice members ko jinke paas ≥10% voting rights hain.
Retiring auditor AGM me letter of representation submit karega.
Members aware honge self-interest, accounting issues, ya koi concern ke bare me.
3️⃣ Casual Vacancy (Causal Vacancy):
Agar auditor withdraw kar de ya continue na kar sake (self-interest, family issue, etc.), casual vacancy hoti hai.
Commission new auditor appoint karega agar company 30 din me fill na kare.
Company ko consent communicate karna aur regulators ko notify karna hoga.
4️⃣ Engagement Letter:
Engagement tabhi start hoti hai jab consent aur resources verify ho jayein audit perform karne ke liye.
Professional reasons (conflict of interest, inadequate resources) acceptance rok sakte hain.
Engagement letter sab checks clear hone ke baad issue hoti hai.
5️⃣ Key Checks Before Appointment:
Self-interest, conflicts of interest, family ties.
Resources aur capability audit perform karne ke liye.
Prior working papers review (agar applicable ho).
Representation letter previous auditor se.
🔹 Topic: Auditor appointment situations, withdrawal, confidence & resolution process.
1️⃣ 30 Days Requirement & Situations:
Agar auditor confident hai aur client ke liye zaruri hai, to 30 din ke andar appointment ho jati hai.
Agar auditor withdraw kar de ya report nahi de, aur big firms refuse karein, to new auditor appoint hota hai.
Client ki galti nahi, auditor ki professional duty ke liye report complete karni hoti hai.
2️⃣ Engagement Letter & Disclosure:
Auditor engagement letter me likhta hai ke koi aise words use nahi honge jo issues disclose karein.
Auditor disclose karega kyun accept kiya engagement aur koi conflicts ya issues nahi hain.
Working days aur practical scenarios ke basis pe audit perform hoti hai.
3️⃣ Payment & Independence:
Auditor free me kaam karega agar company free me appoint kar rahi ho.
Agar payment de raha ho, auditor independence aur consent check karega.
Recommendation board ke taraf se jati hai; board khud appoint kare to process follow hota hai.
4️⃣ Resolution for Appointment & Removal:
New auditor appoint karne ke liye notice bhejna zaruri hai.
Notice minimum days pehle members ko jana chahiye.
Members ke paas ≥10% voting rights honi chahiye propose aur resolution pass karne ke liye.
Special business, capital issue ya shareholder approval ki requirement follow karni hoti hai.
🔹 Topic: Special business for auditor appointment/removal, resolutions, and auditor rights.
1️⃣ Special Business & Voting Power:
Special business includes association/articles change, winding up, etc.
Members ki voting power check karni hai, minimum 10% hona chahiye for proposing an auditor.
Resolution pass hota hai majority (>50%) ya shares ki voting power ke basis pe.
Auditor appointment/removal jaise special business ke liye special resolution zaruri ho sakta hai.
2️⃣ Representation by Retiring Auditor:
Retiring auditor ko right hai representation dene ka before AGM.
Representation agenda item announce hone se pehle read karna zaruri.
Auditor ka present hona ensure karta hai transparency aur time waste na ho.
3️⃣ New Auditor Appointment:
AGM approval ke baad company ko registrar / relevant authority ko inform karna hai about new auditor.
Registrar maintain karta hai company records, shareholders, trading activity, etc.
Proper communication ensures regulatory compliance.
4️⃣ Auditor Removal:
Removal ke liye special resolution chahiye before tenure ends.
Removal ki reason regulator (SECP / commission) ko communicate karni zaruri.
Proper intimation with justification ensures legal protection.
Agar auditor remove hota hai to casual vacancy create hoti hai; new auditor 30 days ke andar appoint karna hota hai.
5️⃣ Auditor Rights & Documentation:
Access to books, accounts, vouchers, aur audit ke liye zaruri data.
Engagement letters (English letters) se legal rights aur responsibilities clear hoti hain.
Client legally provided access deny nahi kar sakta; auditor document kar sakta hai refusal ya non-cooperation.
Regulatory protection ensures auditor ko valid reason ke bina remove ya block nahi kiya ja sakta.
Agar client withdraw karta hai ya cooperation nahi deta, auditor ki duty aur engagement letters cover karti hain.
Auditor ke paas right hai verify karna financial framework, access data, aur ensure karna compliance.
🔹 Topic: Differences between Pakistan reports & Azeza reports, scope, limitations, penalties, and duties of auditor.
1️⃣ Scope Limitation & Penalty:
Agar management ne scope limit impose kiya hai, to penalty auditor par aa sakti hai.
Legal protection auditor ke liye specific sections provide karte hain.
Scope limitation aur penalty ka relation samajhna zaruri hai.
2️⃣ Audit Report Differences:
Pakistan reports aur Azeza reports me farq hai.
Farq mainly duties of auditor aur additional reporting items ki wajah se hai.
Auditor ki duty: dono reports ke liye format aur standard follow karna.
3️⃣ Reporting & Documentation:
Audit report format defined hai 2017/2018 ke standards ke mutabiq.
Auditor ko report me additional items identify karne hain aur unka placement sahi karna hai.
Har report proper read karna zaruri hai pehle, phir hi additional items add karne.
Example: Accounts, zakat, investments, operational reporting, branch records.
4️⃣ Evidence & Adequate Records:
Auditor ensure kare ke financial statements aur accounting records se evidence match ho.
Branch level returns bhi collect karna padta hai, chahe direct audit na ho.
ID card records aur branch records ka match ensure karna.
5️⃣ Auditor’s Duty & Compliance:
Audit ki duty hai ke all relevant documents verify kare.
Ensure kare ke company ke final statements aur accounting records sahi ho.
Auditor ki responsibility: proper evidence collect karna aur report prepare karna according to standards.
🔹 Topic: Auditor reporting on financial statements, AGM, aur opinion paragraph kaise likha jata hai.
1️⃣ Financial Statements & AGM Preparation:
AGM se pehle, company prepare karti hai financial statements (balance sheet, P&L, cash flow).
Auditor review karta hai FS aur report karta hai findings jo members ko AGM me present ki jati hain.
Board pehle approve karta hai FS before presentation.
Auditor report karta hai sabhi changes, disclosures, aur notes jo FS me hain.
2️⃣ Information & Explanation Collection:
Auditor ko collect karni hai information aur explanations best of knowledge & belief ke hisaab se.
Report me mention hona chahiye information obtain hui ya nahi.
Agar additional info available ho to opinion section me include karo.
Isse transparency aur auditing standards ki compliance ensure hoti hai.
3️⃣ Opinion Paragraph & Reporting Standards:
Opinion paragraph business opinion nahi hai, ye audit evidence pe based hai.
Include hota hai P&L, changes in equity, notes, accounting policies.
Auditor confirm karta hai ki FS banaye gaye hai applicable accounting & reporting standards ke mutabiq Pakistan me (SIFRS, Companies Act).
Local FS opinion aur international auditing opinion me fark samajhna zaruri hai (e.g., AIZAH 700 report vs Pakistani reporting).
4️⃣ Audit Evidence & Adequacy:
Auditor ensure karta hai ki sabhi accounting records adequate hain.
Evidence from head office & branches FS se match hona chahiye.
Branch operations (e.g., AJ&K, Dubai branches) included hone chahiye agar consolidated FS ka hissa hain.
Agar misstatements ya missing evidence ho to flag karna.
5️⃣ Regulatory Compliance & Reporting:
Auditor confirm karta hai ki FS Company Act & SIFRS ke mutabiq hain.
Company se required info (e.g., individual amounts >5 lakh) capture karni hai.
Reports compliance aur deviations highlight karti hain, taki members correct info receive karein.
🔹 Topic: Auditor reporting, SECP requirements, and auditor qualifications.
1️⃣ Information Disposal & Confirmation:
Auditor confirms information about disposal of assets (kis ko becha aur kitna).
Ensure company confirms info in report.
Opinion section: highlight fee, balance sheet, income surplus/deficit, EMP.
Opinion mapping: pahla aur second paragraph kaise likhna hai.
2️⃣ Impact of Reporting & SECP Requirements:
Agar company Z ka question aaye, report me kya impact hoga.
Opinion paragraph me impact on FS explain karna.
SECP ki reports: auditor sirf information deta hai, opinion nahi.
Reporting should cover all regulatory requirements and additional requirements.
3️⃣ Order of Information in Report:
Directors’ report covers company functions.
Annual report: shows financial statements.
Information para in audit report: no opinion given; just disclosure.
Differentiate between audit opinion & information para.
4️⃣ Form of Report (English Letter):
Letter shows form/content of audit report.
Auditor specifies under which application/reporting regulation FS prepared.
Report may include events, changes; form updates accordingly.
5️⃣ Auditor Qualifications & Restrictions:
Qualification in auditor order shows who can be auditor.
Person involved in loan or business conflict may not qualify.
Foreign offense: 10-year disqualification from auditing.
Auditor cannot audit company if self-interested or conflict exists.
Ethics and financial interest considerations: critical for auditor independence.

🔹 Audit Restrictions
Agar kuch specific conditions hain toh aap company ka audit nahi kar sakte. ⚠️
Agar disqualified situation ho, toh audit karna allowed nahi.
🔹 Qualification of Auditor
Auditor ke liye AC pass / CPA / CA hona zaruri hai ✅
Chartered Accountant (CA) hona chahiye, jo ki public/private company ke liye eligible hai.
🔹 Company Types
Public Company
Listed ya unlisted ho sakti hai.
Listed public company ka audit CA karega jo valid certificate & practice ke saath ho 💡
Private Company
Agar subsidiary of public company hai, toh audit bhi CA with valid certificate & practice karega.
Agar share capital >= 3 million, same rules apply.
Agar share capital < 3 million, fir CA, Management Accountant (with practice) dono eligible ho sakte hain.
🔹 Auditor License Requirement
CA first obtain kare valid certificate of practice from ICAP (institute) before audit.
Management Accountant ke liye bhi valid practice certificate hona zaruri hai.
🔹 Form & Appointment
Multiple CAs ho sakte hain, lekin jo sign karega, appointment usi ke naam se hogi.
Form of Chartered Accountant / Management Accountant hi audit kar sakta hai.
🔹 Appointment Rules
Audit appointment should be in the name of the firm, signed by the partner 🖊️
Engagement partner responsible for audit, even if multiple partners present ✅
Individual proprietors can open a firm, but audit appointments must meet qualification requirements
🔹 Qualification Requirements
Partner in firm must have qualification for relevant company audit 💡
Private company with share capital >= 3M: Only Chartered Accountant (CA) with valid certificate & practice can audit
ICMA forms: Management Accountants can audit private companies under certain conditions, with valid practice certificate
🔹 Disqualification Rules ⚠️
Auditor cannot audit a company if:
Auditor is/was director in the company in last 3 years
Auditor is/was officer in the company in last 3 years
Auditor is/was employee in the company in last 3 years
Even if auditor is qualified, partner relationships matter:
If auditor’s partner is director, officer, or employee in company, auditor is disqualified
🔹 Family Restrictions ⚠️
Auditor cannot audit company if related family members (e.g., spouse) are directors
Appointment must maintain independence and avoid conflict of interest

🔹 Independence Rules
Auditor kisi company ka audit nahi kar sakta agar usne us company se loan liya ho 💸
Loan lene se auditor dependent ho jata hai company pe, jo independence ko affect karta hai ⚠️
Exception: Agar loan normal course of business me liya gaya ho (jaise bank se lending), toh auditor company ka audit kar sakta hai ✅
🔹 Example Cases
Auditor ne direct company se loan liya → audit nahi kar sakta ❌
Auditor ne bank se loan liya (normal business lending) → audit kar sakta hai ✅
Auditor ko discounted ya preferential loan mila → independence affect ho sakti hai ❌
Auditor ne kisi aur ke liye guarantee di ya security provide ki → cannot audit ⚠️
🔹 Engagement Partner Rule
Engagement partner ko independence maintain karni zaruri hai
Agar engagement partner ne loan liya (non-business lending) → audit nahi kar sakta
Independence check sab partners pe apply hota hai, sirf signing partner nahi 💡
🔹 Loan & Independence Rules
Agar auditor ne company se loan liya hai, wo audit nahi kar sakta ❌
Agar loan normal course of business (like bank lending) hai → audit allowed ✅
Loan kisi aur party ke liye ya third party guarantee de di ho → audit prohibited ⚠️
🔹 Business Relationship Rules
Agar auditor ya uske close family member ko major portion of business ya insurance services company se mila ho → audit restricted
Direct ya indirect business relation → audit not allowed ❌
Agar insurance ya business service normal course of business me hai aur minor hai → allowed ✅

🔹 Corporate Auditor Rules
Koi bhi body corporate auditor nahi ho sakti ❌
Sirf individual practicing CA jiske paas valid Certificate of Practice ho, audit kar sakta hai ✅
Companies apni khud ki auditing nahi kar sakti (no inter-company audit) ❌
🔹 Code of Ethics
Auditors ko full Code of Ethics adopt karna zaruri hai
Agar conflict ho → follow COPE minimum criteria
Material transactions ko assess karo before audit
🔹 Examples
Manufacturing company audit → independent auditor required
Financial institution audit → agar normal business transaction hai, allowed ✅
Insurance company providing minor services → allowed, major portion hai toh restricted ⚠️
🔹 Family & Financial Interest Rules
Auditor ka ya uske spouse ya minor children ka shareholding → auditor khud audit nahi kar sakta ❌
Audit disqualification extends to holding & subsidiaries
Appointment ho gayi aur phir disqualification aayi → audit office vacated
Non-compliance → audit authority action
🔹 Practical Steps
Jo shares audit appointment se pehle hold ho → disclose immediately
Auditor ko 90 days ke andar shares dispose karne honge after appointment
Major financial interest → audit restricted
Minor financial interest → allowed under normal business course ✅
