Lecture- 1

📚 LECTURE FULL EXPLANATION:
Pehle ISA 240 (Fraud) ka review hua tha, jisme dekha ke misstatement fraud se ho sakti hai ya kisi aur wajah se. Related party transactions ka bhi audit mein review zaruri hai.
Objectives:
Related party kya hoti hai samajhna.
Related party ka audit mein role samajhna.
Related party ke transactions ko audit mein kaise identify aur report karte hain.
Definition of Related Party:
Jo person ya entity company se judi ho jiska audit ho raha hai.
Director of company ✅
Major shareholder (80% ya zyada ownership) ✅
Common director dono companies mein → dono related parties ✅
Parent-subsidiary relationship
Close family members of directors
Management personnel (department heads ya decision makers)
Examples / Scenarios:
Single person controls 2 companies → dono companies related parties.
Company ne apne funds se doosri company mein investment ki → subsidiary → related party.
25% ya zyada ownership → associate party
Common control ya influence → related parties
Audit Points:
Focus control & influence par, choti ownership par nahi.
Parent-subsidiary control se entities related.
Management personnel bhi related parties hain.
Related party transactions identify aur evaluate karna zaruri hai.
Practical Understanding:
Related parties sirf ownership par nahi, control, influence, aur family ties par bhi depend karte hain.
Close family members consider karna important.
Common directorship ek critical indicator hai.
Examples for Clarity:
Director dono companies own karta hai → dono related.
Parent company controls subsidiary → dono related.
25% ownership → associate related party
Close Family Members 👪
Close family members (father, son, spouse) bhi related party ke under aate hain.
Example: Director ke close family members bhi company se related hain.
Provident/Pension Fund Example 💰
Company employees ke liye provident fund banati hai aur separate register maintain karti hai.
Company control karti hai fund ka money aur contribution deduct karti hai employees ki salary se.
Entry alag hoti hai aur audit bhi alag hota hai.
Transaction Flow Example: 📝
Salary 100 units → 90 units employee ko pay, 10 units provident fund me.
Fund ka control company ke paas hai → fund bhi related party hai.
Jo bhi pension/provident fund company manage kare → company ke liye related party.
Management Personnel 👨‍💼
Department heads aur decision makers bhi related party ke category me aate hain.
Understanding Relationship & Transactions 🔄
Related party transactions ka audit mein samajhna zaruri.
Low interest ya special transactions me fraud ka risk hota hai.
Market value vs. present value ko compare karna important hai.
Practical Examples 🌐
US subsidiary & Pakistan company ke contracts.
Revenue recording aur outsourcing example: US company contract karti, lekin revenue Pakistan company ke name pe record hota.
Company ke liye ye transactions related party ke under aate hain.
Key Audit Insight 🔍
Focus: Control aur influence, not just ownership.
Related party ke transactions audit me complex structure create karte hain, dikkat hoti hai detect karne me.
Simulated-financial reporting aur related party transactions par focus 📝
Client ne revenue ko proper frame ke under record nahi kiya 😕
Audit mein dekha ke Simulated method adopt hua hai, jisme actual revenue aur invoices misreport hui 💸
Understanding Related Party & Transactions:
Pehle relationship samjho, phir transactions evaluate karo 🔍
Related party ke transactions normal business sense banate hain ya nahi 🤔
Example: PP (Property & Projects) ke rahi hui transactions aur overvaluation 🏢
Audit Observations:
Related party transactions ko hide ya modify kiya ja sakta hai
Transactions ka commercial sense check karna zaruri hai ✅
Agar disclosure nahi hua, to audit ke liye risk create hota hai ⚠️
Examples & Case Scenarios:
Director ki daughter ya favoured party ke saath transactions
Subsidy loan example: subsidized loan dena, normal business practice me nahi 🤷‍♂️
Inventory transactions: cheap purchase & high resale 🏷️
Rent-free space: misreporting in PP disclosures 🏠
Audit Techniques:
Identify hidden or complex transactions 🔍
Evaluate all related party disclosures
Verify investment registers: check ownership % in various companies 📊
Related Party Disclosures aur Accounting 🧾
Har transaction ka disclosure zaruri hai, especially related parties ke liye 📌
Accounting entries related party transactions ke liye alag se maintain karo ✅
Audit Procedures & Investigation:
Hidden ya undisclosed transactions identify karna 🔍
Investment register verify karo: kis company me kitna ownership hai, koi bhi association hide na ho 📊
Check if customer ya company ko kam rate pe provide kiya, proper approval & commercial substance ke sath 🏢
Examples & Case Scenarios:
Non-disclosure impact: chhoti ya large transactions, dono material ho sakti hain ⚠️
Shareholder register ka review: identify major shareholders aur control over company 🧩
Employee funds, contributions: ensure proper accounting and disclosure 💰
Real estate & land: ownership aur usage ka verification 🏠
Audit Techniques:
Transactions ko normal business practice ke against check karo 🔍
Investment aur shareholding records verify karo for completeness and accuracy 📈
Identify unreported transactions and segregate from normal transactions 🧹
Lecture- 2

📚 LECTURE FULL EXPLANATION:
Concept Breakdown ✨
Provident Fund transaction:
Employer → Provident fund → Employee withdrawal.
Yeh Related Party Transaction nahi hai 🚫
Reason: yeh transaction employee aur fund k darmiyan hai, company directly involve nahi hai.
Related Party ka basic rule:
Related party tab banta hai jab direct link / relation ho company aur doosri party me.
Agar employee apna paisa withdraw kar raha hai fund se → not related party.
Important Point:
Agar company provident fund disclose hi na kare → yeh non-compliance hai ❗ but phir bhi related party nahi banti.
Key Check:
Jab tak company aur related person/entity k darmiyan transaction na ho → related party consider nahi hogi.
Disclosure Importance 📌
Related party transactions ko clearly disclose karna lazmi hai.
Example: Director ki beti ko land nominal value pe dena → Related Party Transaction ✅
Agar disclose na ho → misstatement risk hoga.
Fraud Connection 🔍
Related party transactions fraud k liye use ho sakti hain:
Sales unusual terms pe dikhana 📉
Loan ko revenue bana kar show karna 💰
Complex group structures (subsidiaries, offshore entities 🌍)
Auditor ko hamesha check karna hai: transaction ka substance over form.
Examples 🎯
Provident Fund → Not related party transaction.
Director’s daughter ko land at nominal value → Related party transaction.
Extended credit period (90 days vs normal 20 days) → Related party ka indication ho sakta hai.
Subsidiary ko sale aur phir wapas buy back → Related party aur fraud ki possibility.
Main Theme 🌟
Sir ka main point: Understand & Identify Related Party Relationships & Transactions.
Har related party relation clear hona chahiye → account kholna, loan dena, disclosure karna.
Agar yeh samajh na aya → risk of fraud aur misstatement high hai ⚠️.
Core Concept 🔑
Related Party Transaction (RPT) = Transaction between reporting entity & related party.
Sir ne stress kiya: koi third-party transaction ko RPT nahi samjhna.
Focus karo: pehle identify karo related party hai ya nahi, phir transaction evaluate karo ✅.
Examples & Clarifications 🎯
Provident Fund
Fund aur employee k darmiyan withdrawal → Not RPT 🚫
Fund aur company k darmiyan transaction → RPT ✅
Employee vs Company
Employee company ka employee hai → relation defined hai.
Lekin agar employee ki personal deal kisi third-party se hai → Not RPT.
Disclosure ka Focal Point 📌
Related party relation aur transactions ki transparent disclosure honi chahiye.
Agar company ne disclose nahi kiya → misstatement risk ⚠️.
Auditor’s Independence 🛡️
Sir ne example diya: client ne gym membership, bungalow offer kiya.
Point: auditor ko independence compromise nahi karni chahiye.
Code of Ethics follow karna mandatory hai ✔️.
Main Theme 🌟
Focus tha: Identification + Disclosure of RPTs.
Related Party ka matlab: entity aur us k connected persons/organizations.
Agar disclosure incomplete ya hide ho jaye → risk of material misstatement ⚠️.
Core Concepts 🔑
Related Party ko identify karna sirf management ka kaam nahi, auditor ki bhi zimmedari hai.
Auditor ko hamesha professional skepticism rakhnay ki zarurat hai 👀.
RPT disclosure important hai because:
Misstatement ka risk.
Fraud conceal karne k liye use ho sakta hai.
Independence threat ho sakta hai.
Key Clarifications 🎯
Directors’ Loan
Agar director company se loan le → Always Related Party Transaction ✅
Disclosure required hai.
Subsidiary – Parent Deal
Parent aur subsidiary k darmiyan transaction → Always RPT ✅
Example: shares issue, loan advance, purchase/sale.
Third Party Case
Agar director ka dost company k sath deal kare → Not RPT ❌ (jab tak direct/indirect relation prove na ho).
Close Family Members 👨‍👩‍👦
Director ka spouse, children, dependents → Related Party ✅
In k transactions bhi disclose honge.
Key Management Personnel (KMP)
Sir ne bola: CEO, CFO, aur Board members → Always Related Party.

📚 LECTURE FULL EXPLANATION:
🔹 Related Party Relationships & Transactions kyu samajhna zaroori hai?
Framework kehta hai auditor ko check karna chahiye ke RPTs properly account & disclose hue hain ✅
Auditor ki responsibility hai ensure kare ke koi unusual ya outside normal course transaction na ho 🚨
Risk: Related parties ka use fraud hide karne ya manipulate karne k liye ho sakta hai (kyunke control hota hai).
🔹 Auditor ke Initial Steps
Management k pass jao → bolo mujhe list of related parties do 👥
Name of RP
Relationship nature
Year me hui transactions
Year-end outstanding balances
Agar management refuse kare → Auditor ka jawab:
“Meri audit responsibility hai RPT samajhna” 💡
🔹 Auditor ke Objectives RPTs me
Dekhna ke relationships & transactions exist karte hain ya nahi.
Ensure karna ke koi misstatement ya fraud suggest na ho.
Prior year se compare karna ➝ nayi/missing RPs identify karna 🔍
🔹 Completeness Check
Pichle saal ki RP list ko current year se compare karo.
Agar koi party missing ho to inquire karo ❓
Isse nayi aur missing RPs detect hoti hain ✅
🔹 Risks Sir ne bataye ⚠️
Risk of undisclosed related parties.
Risk ke RPTs sahi account/disclose na hue ho.
Example: revenue record na karna through RPT.
🔹 Management ke Controls (Sir ne discuss kiye)
Code of Conduct – KMP disclose karte hain apne relations 👨‍💼
Whistleblowing mechanism – agar kisi ko pata chale manager apne relative k sath transact kar raha hai to report kar sakta hai 📢
Dedicated responsible person – ek officer (aksar Co. Secretary) jo poore saal RPTs track karta hai 📝
Investment Register monitoring – check karna ke company ne director/management linked entity me invest to nahi kia 📊
Cross-check statutory filings – company returns/statements dekh kar confirm karna 🏛️
✨ Key Point: Related Parties = High Fraud Risk Area → Auditor ko hamesha vigilant rehna chahiye!
🌟 Basic Understanding
Related Party ka matlab simple terms me: jab director appoint hota hai, to uski declaration li jati hai ✅
Code of Corporate Governance → Companies par apply hota hai → governance ke liye bana hua.
Associate / Close Family Members sab declare karne hote hain aur yeh documents SECP ke paas file hote hain 📂
🔍 Inquiry & Controls
Inquiry karna zaroori hai related party ke relation aur transactions ka.
Dekhna hota hai:
Nature of relationship (common directorship, ownership, control).
Type of transaction (sale, purchase, rent, investment, etc.).
Business sense banta hai ya nahi? 🤔
Important Line: "Control lagana alag baat hai, uska operate hona alag baat hai" ⚡
🏢 Internal Audit Department
Role: Monitoring of controls.
Check karta hai ke controls functional hain ya sirf design me ache hain.
Related party transactions ke liye inquiry aur verification bhi karta hai.
🗂️ Documentation & Regulatory Filings
Sources of check:
Investment register 📑
Shareholder register 📝
SECP filings ⚖️
Bank confirmations 🏦
Directors’ declarations 📜
📊 Booking & Accounting
Related party ke liye separate ledger codes assign kiye jate hain.
Eg: Director ko salary → us ledger me book hogi → verify hogi from shareholder approval.
Har transaction ka supporting document hona chahiye.
🛡️ Approval Mechanism
Normal transactions → Maker-checker approval.
Related party transactions → Audit Committee ke approval ke through ✅
Audit Committee ka Chairman independent hota hai aur wo directly Board ko report karta hai.
Code of Conduct me likha hota hai ke agar disclose nahi kiya to penal action ho sakta hai ⚠️
💡 Examples
Director ko salary → shareholder approval se fix.
Related party ko asset rent pe dena → transaction declare + approval lena.
Vendor selection → agar apne relative ko select kiya → quotations, quality, history sab check karna hoga ✅
📝 Key Controls
Communication & Policies – clear channel hona chahiye.
Documentation – declaration, registers, SECP filings.
Approval – Audit Committee ki permission.
Periodic Reviews – Internal Audit se time-to-time verification.
Main Theme 🌟
Focus: Control mechanisms, documentation & monitoring of Related Party Transactions (RPTs).
Goal: Ensure ke saari related party relationships aur transactions properly identify, monitor, approve, aur disclose hoon.
Key Concepts 🔑
Director Declarations 📝
Directors aur KMP apne close family members aur doosre related parties ki declarations submit karte hain.
Purpose: Transparency aur regulatory compliance ensure karna.
Code of Corporate Governance 📜
Company ke liye defined hai.
Purpose: Proper identification, approval, aur disclosure of RPTs.
Regulatory filings maintain kiye jate hain (SECP aur statutory records).
Internal Controls 🛡️
Internal order/department monitor karta hai ki:
Control properly design hua hai.
Transactions sahi tarah execute ho rahi hain.
Unauthorized RPT na ho.
Example: Normal purchase/sale/bank transactions verify karna.
Inquiry Process 🔍
Auditor/internal team check karta hai:
Transaction ka type (sale, purchase, rent, salary).
Relationship ka nature.
Documentation complete hai ya nahi.
Purpose: Samajhna ke transaction business purpose serve kar rahi hai ya personal benefit.
Approval Mechanism ✅
Sensitive RPTs ke liye high-level approval (Board/Audit Committee) mandatory.
Normal transactions me maker-checker principle apply hota hai.
Approval ke bina transaction record nahi ho sakti.
Documentation & Registers 📂
Director/KMP declarations.
Transaction ledger / booking codes.
Investment register, shareholder register.
Cross-company holdings, common directorship, aur control percentages verify karna.
Periodic Review 🔄
Internal audit monthly/quarterly checks karta hai.
Sample selection from shareholder/investment register.
RPT transactions ki completeness aur accuracy verify hoti hai.
Practical Application 🏢
Example: Director salary, rent, land transactions.
Ledger me booking entries check ki jati hain.
Financial statements me sirf approved transactions reflect hoti hain.
Audit committee review aur approve karti hai before recording.
Ethical & Regulatory Compliance ⚖️
Code of Conduct / Internal Ethics policies follow ki jati hain.
Warning mechanism: Non-compliance par action trigger hota hai.
Saari communications proper channels ke through hoti hain.
Control Story Summary 📖
Ek responsible person assign kiya jata hai to collect related party data.
Dekha jata hai ki control sahi design aur operate ho raha hai ya nahi.
Regular inquiry ke through transaction types, nature, aur justification verify hoti hai.
High-level approvals ensure karte hain ke koi unauthorized transaction record na ho.
Monthly records aur registers maintain kiye jate hain for audit and review purposes.
Ye process prevent karta hai fraud aur ensure karta hai ke RPTs material misstatement se free hain.
Main Theme 🌟
Focus: Business inquiry, response validation & control mechanisms in RPTs.
Goal: Understand kaise business rationale identify karte hain, responses validate karte hain, aur internal controls ensure karte hain.
Key Concepts 🔑
Business Inquiry & Rationale 🔍
Jab internal inquiry hoti hai, audit team poochti hai: “What is the business rationale?” har transaction ke liye.
Example: Purchase discount, credit period, vendor selection.
Goal: Determine karna ki transaction genuinely business-driven hai ya personal benefit ke liye.
Response Validation ✅
Response aaya, lekin usay blindly accept nahi karte.
Auditor facts compare karta hai aur check karta hai:
Purchase hua ya nahi.
Discount valid tha ya fabricated.
Credit period justified tha ya nahi.
Agar response incomplete ya incorrect, further investigation initiate hoti hai.
Transaction Checking & Classification 🗂️
Purchase, rent, salary, ya land transactions ko filter karke verify karna.
Real-life example: Purchase table create karke lowest aur highest transactions compare karna.
Objective: Correct categorization of related party transactions ensure karna.
Control Mechanisms & Checks 🛡️
Internal controls ensure karte hain ki:
Transactions correct aur authorized hain.
Maker-checker process properly follow ho raha hai.
Accounting entries aur documentation complete hain.
Example: Monthly records compare karna with shareholder register, investment register.
Team Coordination & Responsibilities 👥
Specific individuals responsible hote hain:
Related party data collect karna.
Transactions compare karna.
Discrepancies report karna.
Periodic reviews aur cross-verification team ke through.
Practical Follow-up 🏢
Scenario: Audit team response receive → validate → challenge if required → findings document.
Objective: Identify hidden transactions ya control failures.
Ensure responses align with business logic aur actual records.
Documentation & Evidence 📂
Sab inquiries, responses aur findings document karte hain.
Audit trail build karna easier hota hai related party transactions ke liye.
Example: Excel tables, filtered ledgers, vendor-wise analysis.
Business Sense & Materiality Assessment ⚖️
Check karna ki transaction genuine business need fulfill karti hai ya nahi.
Materiality (financial impact) aur risk evaluate karna.
Decisions hamesha facts par based hon, assumptions par nahi.

📚 LECTURE FULL EXPLANATION:
1️⃣ Related Party Standard & Objective 🎯
Main goal: Related party transactions ko identify, record aur disclose karna.
Ensure karna ke auditing aur checks properly ho rahe hain.
Reason: Related party transactions complex hoti hain aur fraud ka risk hota hai.
2️⃣ Complexity & Fraud Risk ⚠️
Related party transactions design ki jati hain jahan fraud ya misstatement ka chance ho.
Standard 240 se link karke risk factors check karna important hai.
Relevant disclosures observe karne se compliance easy ho jati hai.
3️⃣ Inquiry & Business Understanding 🔍
Management se inquiry karna aur transactions compare karna zaroori hai.
Missing ya unusual transaction detect karna.
Business rationale samajhna, jaise unusual discount ka reason.
4️⃣ Out of Control Management ❌
Identify karna ke kahan management ka control fail hua.
Exam tip: “What is out of control management in ensuring related party relationship & transaction?”
Example: Internal approvals aur monitoring mechanisms verify karna.
5️⃣ Documentation & Monitoring 📄
Maintain internal orders, periodic monitoring, regulatory filings aur director declarations.
Purpose: Ensure related party transaction correct mapping, no deletion or missing.
6️⃣ Normal Course of Business vs Related Party 🏢
Out of normal course of business transactions with related party ka focus fraud aur compliance check pe.
Example: Sale with unusual discount to related party, justify business rationale.
7️⃣ Practical Approach 🔧
Step 1: Inquire management and understand rationale.
Step 2: Compare with normal course of business.
Step 3: Ensure documentation, approvals, monitoring mechanisms.
Step 4: Identify potential fraud or unusual transaction design.
1️⃣ Out of Normal Course of Business Transactions 📊
Identify karna ke related party ke through koi transaction normal course ke bahar ho.
Signal milne ke baad agreement inspect karna aur business rationale samajhna zaroori.
Purpose: Fraud risk detect karna aur ensure karna ki transaction genuine hai.
Example: High value plant import jo Dubai se hui, aur transaction normal course ke bahar thi.
2️⃣ Agreement Inspection & Matching 🔍
Agreement aur management explanations match karni chahiye.
Check karna ke high value purchases aur related party transactions properly documented hain.
Steps:
Agreement ko inspect karo.
Transaction aur management explanation ko match karo.
Identify karo agar discrepancy hai.
3️⃣ Accounting & Recording 💰
Correct accounting treatment ensure karna essential hai.
Loan aur payment treatment properly record karna zaroori.
Example: High value PP (Property, Plant) ka cost plus contract terms ke mutabiq record karna.
Goal: Transaction accurate, transparent aur audit ready ho.
4️⃣ Management Inquiry & Controls 🛠️
Management se poochna ki transaction kyun hui aur approvals ka status kya hai.
Out of control ya unusual transactions identify karna.
Example: Security aur collateral ke percentage verify karna aur control lagana.
5️⃣ Verification & Documentation 📝
Verify karna ke out of normal course transaction properly inline hai ya nahi.
Ensure karna ke related party disclosures aur normal course transactions match kar rahe hain.
Agar offline transactions hain, to reason aur approvals check karna zaroori hai.
6️⃣ Transaction Review Process ⚙️
Steps to follow:
Inspect agreement.
Inquire management aur match explanation.
Verify accounting treatment.
Check approvals, collateral aur security.
Confirm documentation aur related party disclosure.
1️⃣ Online vs Offline Transactions 📑
Online transactions: Financial system me properly record hoti hain.
Offline transactions: Out of normal course of business ke under, manually check karni hoti hain.
Purpose: Verify karna ke management ka claim correct hai aur accounting treatment sahi hai.
Example: Related party transactions jo management ke result se match karni hain.
2️⃣ Audit Verification & Technical Environment 🔧
High technical environment me transaction tracing aur verification complex ho sakta hai.
Large customer base ya auto-recorded income me audit control zaroori.
Scope limitation ko samajhna important hai.
3️⃣ Expert Valuation & Market Assessment 📈
Large transactions ya asset purchase ke liye market value ka assessment zaruri hai.
Expert ko involve karke fair value determine karna.
Example: Recent plant transaction ya high-value assets.
4️⃣ Management Claims & Validation 🧐
Management ka claim verify karna ke transaction aur related party disclosures sahi hain.
Inline transactions aur accounting treatment check karna.
Discrepancy ya fraud ka signal identify karna.
5️⃣ Audit Procedures & Documentation 📝
Audit procedure follow karna:
Customer confirmation.
Sales data aur documentation verify karna.
Approval aur current limit check karna.
Ensure karna ke online aur offline transactions properly documented hain.
